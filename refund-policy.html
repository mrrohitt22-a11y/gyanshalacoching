Refund & Cancellation

Last updated: April 2026

We want our students and parents to be completely at peace with their decision to join Gyan Shala. Please find our complete Refund Policy below.

1. Registration Fee

The initial registration/admission fee paid at the time of enrollment is strictly non-refundable under any circumstances, as it secures the student's seat in our limited-capacity batches.

2. Tuition Fee Refunds

  • If a student decides to withdraw from the coaching within the first 7 days of commencement of classes, a pro-rated refund of the tuition fee for the remaining months will be processed.
  • After 7 days from the start of the batch, no refund requests for the current quarter's tuition fee will be entertained.
  • In cases of medical emergencies requiring long-term absence, parents may submit a written request with medical certificates for a fee adjustment in the next quarter, subject to management approval.

3. Cancellation Process

To cancel an enrollment and apply for an eligible refund, a formal written request must be submitted to the center head at our Ballabhgarh office. Verbal communications will not be considered valid for refunds.

4. Processing Time

Eligible refunds will be processed and credited back to the original source of payment within 10-15 working days from the date of approval.
